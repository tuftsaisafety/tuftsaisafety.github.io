About Us

The Tufts AI Safety Student Association (TASSA) is a student-led organization committed to understanding and reducing the risks of advanced AI systems. We provide students and faculty with resources, events, and career-building opportunities to advance the technical and policy work needed for safe and responsible AI.

Programs

Technical Fellowship

Applications open soon for Fall 2025!

Our technical fellowship is an 8-week, in-person reading group that covers the foundations of frontier AI technology with topics like model interpretability, goal misgeneralization, reinforcement learning, oversight, and more.

The program is self-contained with no outside readings required and catered dinner/lunch is provided. The fellowship is open to undergraduates, graduate students, and postdocs looking to learn more about technical AI safety. Please direct questions to contact@tassa.dev.

Policy Fellowship

Applications open soon for Fall 2025!

Our policy fellowship is an 8-week, in-person reading group that discusses governance and policy solutions for problems posed by transformative AI systems. We will read draft legislation, dissect governance frameworks, and develop a basic understanding of the social, political, and technical challenges of AI.

The program is self-contained with no outside readings required and catered dinner/lunch is provided. The fellowship is open to undergraduates, graduate students, and postdocs looking to learn more about AI policy. Please direct questions to contact@tassa.dev.
