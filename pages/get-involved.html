How to Get Involved

We want to engage all members of the Tufts community. Whether you're an undergraduate, graduate, or doctoral student, faculty or staff, or someone else, here are the best ways to plug in.

Join our Slack

Slack is our primary space for announcements, discussions, and community building. Join to get real-time updates on important events, papers, and (career) opportunities.

Join the Slack and introduce yourself in the #intros channel! Make sure to turn on notifications!

Mailing list

Prefer email? We also share important updates and application links via our mailing list.

Subscribe to the mailing list here.

Fellowships

Each semester we run two fellowships:

  • Technical Fellowship: Technical foundations of frontier AI, including model architecture, interpretability, robustness, oversight, and more.
  • Policy Fellowship: Governance and policy approaches to the most important AI risks.

Both programs are self-contained (no outside readings required) and include meals. Open to undergraduates, graduate students, and postdocs. Questions? contact@tassa.dev.

Attend events

We host talks, reading groups, and community events throughout the semester. You can subscribe to our Luma calendar here: https://luma.com/TASSA.
