Our spring fellowship applications are now open! Apply here by January 25, 2026 (end of day, anywhere on earth).

Our fellowships are 8-week, in-person reading groups that meet for 1.5hrs/wk. Fellows can participate in one or both of the following:

  • Technical Fellowship: Covers model interpretability, goal misgeneralization, reinforcement learning, scalable oversight, red-teaming, and more.
  • Policy Fellowship: Discusses AI governance, responsible scaling, compute governance, deepfakes & misinformation, international frameworks, and more.

Open to undergraduate and graduate students. Catered lunch/dinner from local restaurants is provided.

No prior experience required — we encourage anyone to apply! Learn more on our website.
