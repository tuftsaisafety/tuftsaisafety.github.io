We are excited to announce Tufts AI Safety x Tech@Fletcher: AI Policy Hackathon 2026!

TL;DR: TASSA is hosting a 2-day policy writing event February 7-8th at Eaton Hall. Compete solo or in teams of up to 4 people for a $3K prize pool. Apply here: tassa.dev/policy-hackathon-application. Applications are considered on a rolling basis and close January 28th.

Here's what you need to know:

  • What: A 2-day AI policy writing competition where you'll develop concrete policy proposals to address real-world AI governance challenges. Participants will choose between two tracks: International AI Governance and AI x Cyber/Chem/Bio Risks.
  • Why: Build career capital and network with policy professionals while tackling high-impact problems. You will receive direct feedback on your work from expert judges — to be announced soon!
  • When: February 7-8, 2026
  • Where: Eaton Hall, Tufts University
  • Who: Open to all Boston-area university students. Form teams of up to 4 people or compete solo.
  • Prizes: $3,000 prize pool for winning proposals.
  • Apply: tassa.dev/policy-hackathon-application
  • Add to Your Calendar: luma.com/jgv70ogp
Whether you're passionate about AI risks & harms, interested in policy work, or just want to tackle meaningful challenges with your peers, this hackathon is for you!
